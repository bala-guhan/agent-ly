Company Overview & Mission Statement

Founded: 2019
Headquarters: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India

ABOUT 2CARE:
2Care is a leading healthcare technology company that provides comprehensive digital health solutions to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers across Asia-Pacific. Our cloud-based platform integrates patient management, telemedicine, electronic health records (EHR), and AI-powered diagnostics.

MISSION:
To democratize quality healthcare by making advanced medical technology accessible and affordable to healthcare providers of all sizes.

VISION:
Becoming the #1 healthcare SaaS platform in Asia-Pacific by 2027, serving 10,000+ healthcare facilities and improving outcomes for 50 million patients.

CORE VALUES:
- Patient-First: Every feature designed with patient outcomes in mind
- Innovation: Leveraging AI/ML for better diagnostics and predictions
- Accessibility: Making enterprise-grade healthcare IT affordable for small to large providers
- Compliance: HIPAA, ISO 27001, GDPR, and local healthcare regulations adherence
- Collaboration: Partnering with doctors, hospitals, and medical institutions

PRODUCTS & SERVICES:
1. 2Care Cloud EHR - Electronic Health Records system
