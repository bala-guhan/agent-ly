CASE STUDY 1: METROPOLITAN GENERAL HOSPITAL
================================================================================

Client Profile:
- Organization: Metropolitan General Hospital
- Location: Chicago, Illinois
- Size: 850-bed tertiary care facility
- Staff: 3,200 employees
- Annual Patient Volume: 45,000 admissions

Challenge:
Metropolitan General faced critical challenges with patient data fragmentation across 12 legacy systems, leading to medication errors, duplicate testing, and delayed care decisions. Emergency department wait times averaged 4.5 hours, and patient satisfaction scores were declining.

Solution Implemented:
2care deployed a comprehensive Electronic Health Record (EHR) integration platform with real-time analytics, clinical decision support, and mobile accessibility for physicians and nurses.

Implementation Timeline:
- Planning Phase: 3 months
- Pilot Program: 2 months (2 departments)
- Full Deployment: 8 months
- Training: Ongoing

Results Achieved:
- Emergency department wait times reduced by 42% (from 4.5 to 2.6 hours)
- Medication errors decreased by 78%
- Duplicate test orders reduced by 65%, saving $2.3M annually
- Patient satisfaction scores increased from 72% to 91%
- Physician documentation time reduced by 35%
- Hospital readmission rates decreased by 23%

Financial Impact:
- Total investment: $4.2M
- Annual cost savings: $5.8M
- ROI achieved in 9 months
- Projected 5-year savings: $28M

Key Success Factors:
Strong executive sponsorship, comprehensive staff training program, phased implementation approach, and dedicated 2care support team on-site during transition.
